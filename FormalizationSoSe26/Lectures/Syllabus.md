## Course Material Summary 
Here is a more detailed course summary.

### From AI to Formalization
- AI is coming.
- It still hallucinates. This makes it unreliable for proofs.
- This means we need alternatives.
- So we need an alternative to check correct proofs. The answer is formalization.

### Formalization 
- Formalization translates mathematical statements into computer language and formally verifies them.
- It's as reliable as the kernel it is based on.
- There are various languages that formalize mathematics, such as *Lean*, *Rocq*, *Agda*, ... .

### Lean
- In this class we focus on Lean and particularly *Lean4*.
- Here the math library is known as *MathLib*.
